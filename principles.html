Our Philosophy

A Comprehensive,
Systemic Approach

KBTU's commitment to sustainability goes beyond statements. Our comprehensive approach lies in combining ESG-relevant policies with tangible achievements and clear future plans — through systematic integration of these values into all aspects of the university's activities.

The adoption of these principles reflects the university's commitment to contributing to a sustainable future, ensuring transparency, inclusiveness, and responsible stewardship of the environment and society.

KBTU implements specific, measurable initiatives that demonstrate extensive competencies and genuine leadership in the field of sustainable development not just in Kazakhstan, but across Central Asia and the CIS region.

Three Pillars

Environmental · Social · Governance

Each pillar represents a distinct dimension of KBTU's commitment to building a responsible institution.

🌿
Environmental

Planet & Ecology

KBTU pursues a green campus transformation — energy-efficient technologies, waste segregation, renewable energy, and a carbon neutrality strategy with clear targets and decarbonization roadmaps integrated across research and operations.

🤝
Social

People & Community

Through the Diversity, Equity & Inclusion Policy, student and staff ESG initiatives, and the ESG Campus community, KBTU ensures equal opportunities and actively involves the entire university community in sustainability efforts.

⚖️
Governance

Integrity & Transparency

The Corporate Governance Code, Academic Integrity Policy, and Anti-Corruption Policy establish a framework of transparent, ethical, and accountable governance — setting a standard for responsible institutional management.
